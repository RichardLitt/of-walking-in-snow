Of Walking in Snow

Richard Littauer :: 2016

In February, Richard plans to walk from Boston to the end of Cape Cod. He will walk the 100 miles alone, in the snow. Every day, he will stop into cafes for a few hours to work and blog, answer emails, and prepare the next leg.

He is going for several reasons: to recreate Werner Herzog's walk from Munich to Paris in 1974; to see if digital nomadism is something that can be done on foot; to see if he can learn about his father; and to walk. He will bring along a camera, and will make a documentary film from the footage.
