Of Walking in Snow

Richard Littauer :: 2017

Richard will walk from Boston to the end of Cape Cod. He will walk the 100 miles alone, in the snow. He is going in order to visit his father; to learn about the winter; to recreate Werner Herzog's walk from Munich to Paris in 1974; to see if digital nomadism is something that can be done on foot; to walk.

Every day, he will stop into cafes for a few hours to work and blog, answer emails, and prepare the next leg. Every night, he'll sleep in his bivouac in a park. He's bringing along a camera, and will make a documentary film from the footage. He'll post updates here.

Current status: Postponed due to stress fracture. Planned for Spring 2018..
